comment:1 Changed 7 years ago by graybeal

All, until we start putting content on a wiki you are welcome to add both comments and suggested FAQ questions/categories in this ticket.

First draft of topics for questions, those in quotes are specific topics I've thought of:

CF Conventions - General

  • who/what/when/where/why, only as needed for context
  • 'How to ask questions' and 'How to propose and discuss changes'
  • 'How and when changes are accepted (finalized) and shown in the document'

CF Conventions - Technical

  • big picture concepts that can help understand the conventions
  • 'Relation to netCDF and COARDS conventions'
  • 'Discrete Sampling Geometries and their application'
  • 'UDUNITS and CF' -- how to build and use CF-compatible units

CF Standard Names

  • General and specific information about purpose and mechanisms of standard names
  • 'Components of a standard name' -- name, modifiers, required attributes
  • 'How to find the standard name you need'
  • 'How to request a new standard name'
  • 'How to build a good standard name'
  • 'Examples of common standard name phrases'
  • 'What can be in a standard name?' -- different descriptive categories
  • 'I thought X was the official list of standard names' -- what are the services and how are they different
  • 'Mappings of standard names to other vocabularies'

General strategy is to reference other material when available, but some repetition will be inevitable.
